What is meditation ?



Meditation is an ancient technique, some archaeologists date meditation from the year 5000 a. C., according to Psychology Today. Meditation is a mental practice where a mode of full consciousness is induced. This practice takes us to a state in which we can reduce stress levels and modify our emotions. also, through continuous practice, we can improve our health and well-being. despite the age of this technique. the current pace of life has made many people forget to dedicate time to their inner balance, this happens even in countries with a long tradition in meditation.
